# About PulseStack

PulseStack is a comprehensive employee wellness platform designed to foster a culture of health and engagement within your organization. 

Our mission is to provide employees with the tools they need to track their progress, participate in meaningful challenges, and achieve their personal health goals.

## Core Principles

- **Privacy First**: Your health data is your own. We ensure the highest standards of data security and privacy.
- **Engagement**: Gamified challenges and rewards keep you motivated.
- **Data-Driven**: Real-time analytics help you understand your wellness journey.

## Key Features

- **Activity Tracking**: Seamlessly log your walks, runs, yoga sessions, and more.
- **Wellness Challenges**: Join company-wide challenges to stay active and earn points.
- **Personalized Dashboards**: Visualize your progress with intuitive charts and reports.
- **Community Support**: Connect with colleagues and share your wellness milestones.

## Our Technology

PulseStack is built on a modern, high-performance stack:

- **FastAPI**: A state-of-the-art Python backend for lightning-fast API responses.
- **Next.js & React**: A smooth, responsive frontend experience.
- **MongoDB**: A flexible NoSQL database to handle diverse health data at scale.
- **ODMantic**: For elegant and efficient data modeling.
- **Docker**: Ensured consistency across development and production environments.
